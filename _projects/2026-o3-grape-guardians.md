This was the third step in a project to find a way to stop the invasive spotted lanternflies from hurting vineyard production in New York. In this segment, we narrowed down our proposed solution to a set of lights paired with traps set around the edges of vinetards to reduce their numbers, and pitched our idea to the clients, which in this case are wineries in the area.
